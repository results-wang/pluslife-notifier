Privacy Policy

Approach

Data privacy is important to us. We do not want your data. We collect the minimum data needed to run this service, and delete it as soon as we can.

Data we collect

The data we collect are:

DatumWhy we collect itHow long we keep it for
Email address To be able to send you your results by email. Until we notify you of the result, then we delete it. If we don't get a result for your test, we delete your email address after 7 days. We do internally log your email address for debugging purposes, but don't persist these logs.
PlusLife test data To be able to graph your test results, and email them to you. Until we notify you of the result, then we delete it. If we don't get a result for your test, we delete any uploaded test data after 7 days.

Data processors

Your data is kept/passed in the following places:

  • Our servers are hosted by Hetzner, and are located in Germany. Your data is transmitted over HTTPS from your computer to our servers, which should prevent interception on the way.
  • For sending emails, we use MailGun, specifically their servers in the EU. They may have access to the contents of the emails we send, and the email address it's sent to.
  • Our servers are managed using Coolify Cloud, which has access to control our servers.
